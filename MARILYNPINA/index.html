the process

Step One:
We will discuss your brands goals, expected deliverables, and find a content package that works for both parties. This conversation may happen over a scheduled call, or Email exchange.

Step Two:
A contract will be sent over to you if you do not already provide one. If you do provide a contract, please expect 1-2 business days for the contract to be reviewed. If everything looks good, I will send over the signed contract.

Step Three:
Once product is received, I will shoot and edit the content upon arrival. You will receive the watermarked content to review. From there, you’re able to request revisions and I will reshoot or edit the content according to those requests.

Step Four:
Upon waiting for the product to be delivered, I will conduct brand market research and analyze the following: — Your competitors — Your brand aesthetic — Your target audience During this time I will come up with video concepts based on our previous conversation and my research.

Step Five:
Once the watermarked content is approved, I will go ahead and send over an invoice. The invoice will be sent over where you will be able to select your preferred payment method.(PayPal, Wire Transfer, Etc.)
